02 — Social Media Flow

The Campaign Funnel

1
🔵 LinkedIn + Google Ads
Awareness — Make the Noise
The prospect discovers Prachay Bonds for the first time through targeted ads, organic posts, or a connection's share.
◆LinkedIn carousel ad: "Why 10,000+ investors are moving out of FDs in 2026" — ends with "Learn More" button to landing page
◆Google Search captures: "NCD 13% returns India" → lands on dedicated Prachay Bonds page
◆YouTube 15s pre-roll on personal finance videos: "Your FD is giving 7%. Ours gives 13%. Monthly."
◆Instagram Reel: "3 reasons smart investors are choosing NCDs over FDs" — saves & shares drive organic reach
2
🟣 Instagram + X (Twitter)
Interest — Stop the Scroll
The prospect engages with content — saves a post, clicks a story, follows the account. This signals real intent.
◆Instagram Story Poll: "Are your investments beating 10% p.a.?" — 'No' leads to swipe-up → lead form
◆Twitter Thread: "I put ₹10 Lakhs in Prachay Bonds 12 months ago. Here's what happened." — Viral, link to landing page in thread
◆Instagram Carousel: "NCD vs FD vs Mutual Fund — Full Breakdown [Save This]" — educational, builds trust
◆LinkedIn Article by Girish Lakhotiya: "The Fixed Income Revolution in India" — thought leadership builds credibility
3
🟢 WhatsApp + Email
Capture — Get the Lead
Convert interest into a contact. Every social channel drives to a lead capture — form, WhatsApp opt-in, or webinar registration.
◆LinkedIn Lead Gen Form: "Get Prachay Bonds Prospectus" — auto-fills from LinkedIn profile, no friction
◆Instagram/Facebook: "Register for Free Webinar — Smart Fixed Income Investing 2026" → Zoho form
◆WhatsApp Click-to-Chat link in every bio and post: "Type BONDS to get the product sheet instantly"
◆Landing page: "Calculate your monthly income from ₹[X] in Prachay Bonds" — interactive calculator + form
4
🟡 Email + WhatsApp Sequences
Nurture — Build Trust
Warm the lead with a structured content sequence. Not every prospect invests on day 1 — nurture builds conviction.
◆Day 1 Email: Welcome + NCD Product Sheet + "What makes Prachay different" — warm, personal tone
◆Day 3 WhatsApp: 90-second explainer video "How Prachay Bonds work — in simple terms"
◆Day 5 Email: Investor story — "How [Name] earns ₹43,000/month from Prachay Bonds" (testimonial)
◆Day 8 LinkedIn Retargeting Ad: CRISIL rating + safety features — for trust-seeking leads
◆Day 10 WhatsApp: "You're invited — Live Q&A with our investment team this Saturday"
5
🔴 1:1 Sales + CRM
Qualify — Score & Prioritize
Sales team engages directly. BANT qualification happens over a call, DM, or in-person meeting. CRM updated with lead score.
◆RM sends personalized ROI calculator: "Based on ₹[X], here's your monthly income from Prachay Bonds"
◆Calendar invite sent for 30-min product walkthrough over Zoom or in-person at Prachay Pune office
◆CRM lead scored: Hot (₹5L+ ready now) / Warm (exploring) / Cold (future intent)
◆Hot leads get VIP invite to exclusive Prachay investor breakfast event
6
⭐ Conversion + Referral Loop
Convert + Multiply — Close & Grow
Investor completes KYC and application. Post-investment nurture turns investors into brand ambassadors who refer new leads.
◆Smooth KYC + digital application — completed within 48 hours with dedicated ops support
◆Welcome WhatsApp + Email: "Welcome to the Prachay Family — your first interest arrives on [date]"
◆Month 2: Referral ask — "Refer a friend, earn [incentive]" — WhatsApp + Email CTA
◆Investors share their monthly payout screenshot → organic social proof → new top-of-funnel leads
